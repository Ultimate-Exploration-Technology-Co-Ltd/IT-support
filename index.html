Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe to premium features?

To subscribe to premium features:

  1. Open the Wishes Come True app
  2. Tap the "Subscribe" button
  3. Choose your subscription plan
  4. Confirm your purchase

Note: All subscriptions include a 3-day free trial. You won't be charged until after the trial period ends.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

Step 1: Open Settings on your iPhone
Step 2: Tap your Apple ID at the top
Step 3: Tap "Subscriptions"
Step 4: Find "Wishes Come True"
Step 5: Tap "Cancel Subscription"

Important: Canceling your subscription will stop auto-renewal, but you'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchases?

To restore your previous purchases:

  1. Open the Wishes Come True app
  2. Tap the restore button (usually in the settings or purchase screen)
  3. Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase

Alternatively, you can restore purchases in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

What happens after my free trial ends?

After your 3-day free trial ends:

  • Your subscription will automatically renew
  • You'll be charged the subscription price
  • You'll continue to have access to all premium features
  • You can cancel anytime before the trial ends to avoid charges

I'm having trouble with the app

If you're experiencing issues with the app, please try the following:

  • Make sure you're using the latest version of the app
  • Restart your device
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try uninstalling and reinstalling the app

If the problem persists, please contact us at contact@ultexplor.tech with details about the issue.

How do I manage my wishes?

You can manage your wishes by:

  • Creating new wishes using the "+" button
  • Editing wishes by tapping on them
  • Recording daily progress for each wish
  • Viewing your wish history and progress

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes! Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID, so you can use the app on all devices signed in with the same Apple ID. Your wishes and progress will be stored locally on each device.
