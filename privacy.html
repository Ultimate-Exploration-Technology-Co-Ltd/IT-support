Introduction

Guangzhou Ultimate Exploration Technology Co., Ltd. ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Wishes Come True mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices regarding your use of our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This App is a standalone application that operates entirely on your device without collecting or transmitting your personal information to our servers.

Important: This is a Standalone App

Wishes Come True is a standalone application that runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or app data to our servers or third parties.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Wishes Come True is designed to work completely offline. All data you create in the App, including:

  • Wishes and goals you create
  • Daily progress and journal entries
  • Any other content you input into the App

is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

Payment Information

All in-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or any payment details. Apple handles all payment transactions securely according to their own privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

We do not use your information because we do not collect it.

Since all your data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us, we have no access to your information and therefore cannot use it for any purpose.

Data Storage

Local Storage Only

All your data, including wishes, goals, progress, and journal entries, is stored exclusively on your device in the App's local storage. This data is never:

  • Transmitted to our servers
  • Shared with third parties
  • Backed up to cloud services (unless you use your device's built-in backup features)
  • Accessible to us or anyone else

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. Since the data never leaves your device, it is subject to the same security protections as other data stored on your device.

Note: If you uninstall the App, all your data stored in the App will be deleted from your device.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or disclose any information because we do not collect any information.

Since we do not collect, store, or have access to any of your data, there is no information to share or disclose. Your data remains private and secure on your device.

Third-Party Services

Apple App Store

Our App uses Apple's App Store for in-app purchases and subscriptions. Your use of these services is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We do not have access to your Apple ID or payment information.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or third-party services that collect information about your use of the App. The App operates completely independently without any external data collection.

Your Rights and Choices

Since we do not collect any information, you have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can access and review all your data directly within the App at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your data at any time using the App's delete features, or by uninstalling the App.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information directly within the App.
  • Control: Your data is entirely under your control since it never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no privacy concerns for children. All data created by children using the App remains on their device and is never shared with us or any third parties.

International Data Transfers

No data transfers occur. Since all your data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transmit any information, there are no international data transfers to be concerned about.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
